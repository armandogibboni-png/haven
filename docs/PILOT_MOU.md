## 1. Purpose

This MOU sets out the terms of a one-term pilot of **Haven**, a digital SEN support platform, at [SCHOOL NAME]. The pilot is free of charge and without obligation to continue.

| Item | Detail |

---

## 3. School commitments

The school agrees to:

1. Identify eligible students and obtain parental consent before enrollment
2. Enrol participating teachers (and SNA if applicable) with PIN-protected profiles
3. Ensure at least **1 teacher observation per student per week** during the pilot
4. Complete the **SMQ T0** (baseline) assessment for each enrolled student at pilot start
5. Complete the **SMQ T1** assessment at pilot end
6. Complete the end-of-pilot teacher survey
7. Participate in a mid-pilot check-in call (Week 5)
8. Export all SEN Plans at pilot end for school records

---

## 4. Haven project commitments

The Haven project lead agrees to:

1. Provide a 30-minute onboarding session for participating teachers
2. Share the Teacher Quick-Start Guide with all enrolled teachers
3. Be available by email during the pilot for technical support
4. Conduct a mid-pilot check-in call (Week 5)
5. Produce a pilot evaluation report within 4 weeks of pilot end
6. Not contact students directly under any circumstances
7. Not share any data from the pilot with third parties

---

## 5. Data protection

- All family and student data is stored in the user's browser (localStorage). **No data is stored on Haven's servers.**
- AI chat messages are processed by Anthropic, Inc. (USA) under their [Data Processing Agreement](https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy).
- The school remains the data controller for any educational data entered into Haven.
- Parental consent must be obtained by the school before a child is enrolled.
- Haven's full Privacy Policy is available at [HAVEN URL]/privacy.
- Either party may terminate this pilot at any time. All data can be exported and/or deleted by the user.

---

## 6. Cost

This pilot is provided **free of charge**. No contract or commitment to a paid plan is created by signing this MOU.

At the end of the pilot, the school may:
- Continue on a paid annual plan (from €299/year for up to 10 SEN students)
- Continue on a free basis with limited features
- Discontinue, with no obligation

---

## 7. Safeguarding

Haven is an **educational tool**, not a clinical or crisis service. The school's existing safeguarding and child protection procedures remain in force. If a teacher has concerns about a student's wellbeing, they should follow the school's safeguarding protocol. Haven provides signposting links to NCSE SENO, HSE CAMHS, NEPS, and Tusla via the crisis escalation button (🆘) in the SEN Plan.
